时间都去哪儿了

  • 每天 = 24h

  • 每周 = 7 * 24h = 168h

  • 每年 = 52 * 168h = 8736h

  • 每月 ≈ 8736 / 12 = 728 h

sleep_every_day = 8h
year_sleep = 8h * 7 * 52 = 2912h

work_every_day = 10h
year_work = 10h * 5 * 52 = 2600h

every_year_real_free_time = 8736 - 2912 - 2600 = 3224h
